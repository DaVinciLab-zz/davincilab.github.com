Task1: Please depict the FA values on the tube

This task is to sketch FA values. FA stands for fractional anasotropy which represents how fast water moves in our brain. The faster the water moves, the higher the FA values. FA value is normalized to [0, 1]. In the region where the water is still, the FA value is 0. In our data, the FA values are in the range of [0.02, 0.8].

Please draw the brain dataset shown to clearly depict the FA values. We are especially interested in the regions where two boxes are located. For these two areas, please make sure your drawing allows comparison, i.e., it clearly shows which region between the two may have higher FA values. currently, the FA values are displayed using a color map (therefore, colors is not allowed in your drawing.) The FA color map is shown on the right: the redder the color, the higher the FA value. Please draw as much as possible the rest of the model as well. Task2: Tumor area Task

In this task, You will be shown a sphere embedded in a 3D brain DMRI model. The sphere represents the tumor. You are asked to encode tumor position and the distance from the tumor area with the surrounding tubes in your drawing. We give your a label (you do not need to draw the label in your drawing, but you need to draw the tumor) to show where there is a tumor area, and you may agree it is hard to find the "tumor" without the label . Your drawing could help us easily find the tumor area and the relationship between the tumor with the surrounding tubes, which is no contact, tangent or full penetration. Tangent means that the sphere grazed the tube(s) but did not fully intersect, full intersection means that the sphere intersected the tube(s).
