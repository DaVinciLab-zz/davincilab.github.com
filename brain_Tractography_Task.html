FA tractography Task

In this task, You will be shown two boxes embedded in a FA color-encoded 3D brain DMRI model. The FA color map is shown on the right: the redder the color, the higher the FA value. You are asked to encode FA value information in your drawing for people to easily compare the FA (or average FA value) in certain areas. After finishing, your drawing should clearly show the difference of FA in different area. As we pick up two box (marked 1, marked 2), we can easily know which area has a higher FA value. In your drawing, you should also draw two boxes in your drawing and separately mark 1 and 2.


Tumor area Task

In this task, You will be shown a sphere embedded in a 3D brain DMRI model. The sphere represents the tumor. You are asked to encode tumor position and the distance from the tumor area with the surrounding in your drawing. We give your a label(you do not need to draw the label in your drawing, but you need to draw the tumor) to show you where is the "Tumor area", and you will find without the label it is hard to find this "tumor". We want your drawing could help us easily find the tumor area and the relationship (no contact, tangent, full penetration Tangent means that the sphere grazed the tube(s) but did not fully intersect,full intersection means that the sphere intersected the tube(s) ) between the tumor with the surrounding tubes.


Lesion Area task

In this task, You will be shown a box embedded in a 3D brain DMRI model. There are no tubes inside the box which represents the lesion area. The box and a label just serves as hint to help you find the lesion area, However, your drawing do not need to drawing the box, just with no tube in that area. Your drawing should encode the position of the lesion area for people to easily to find the lesion area(some part of tubes is missing).


Tube Orientation Task

In this task, You will be showed three boxes embedded in a 3D brain DMRI model and with a set of Red big sphere marks textured in some endpoints of tubes. The Red big spheres show the starting point of the tube(s), while the three boxes represent the possible endpoints, those hints give you the goal of your drawing, which is easily to show the orientation informaiton of the tubes. With this task, people can easily find the endpoints from the three box. we gives the right answer with one box has Red big spheres. You should drawing the starting big Red Sphere and the 3 Box, you do not need to draw the Big red sphere inside the box.


Same Bundle Task

In this task, You will be showed a 3D brain DMRI model with some fiber tracts marked in yellow. You are asked to convey the fiber bundle information in the drawing. The yellow fiber bundles is possible from the same bundle. About 50% of the data are in the same bundle and 50% are not. Your drawing could separate the five major fiber bundles, so that we could easy to judge whether part of the fiber are the same bundle or not?


Same Bundle Task

In this task, You will be shown a blue CST bundle and a CC bundle embedded in a 3D brain DMRI model, and You are asked to encode depth information in the drawing. When seeing the model, you may find it is hard to assess the distance from the viewer to the tube and the depth relationship of the tubes. You drawing should help in showing the depth relation of tube(s) we choose two bundle (CST and CC), we can easily know the depth relation of those two bundles and the individual tube in each bundle.
