Welcome and thank you for agreeing to participate in this study.

By taking part in this study, you will be helping us learn ways to visually represent complex brain spatial structures from diffusion MRI. The results of this study will eventually be used to design new algorithms to help brain scientists examine pathological conditions related to disease treatment and diagnosis.

The study will include four parts (1) Background survey, (2) Training, (3) the Drawing experiment, and (4) a Post-questionnaire.
