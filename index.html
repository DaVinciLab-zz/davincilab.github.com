Welcome and thank you for agreeing to participate in our study.
By taking part in this study, you will be helping us learn ways to visually present complicated spatial structures of our brains. We will use the knowledge learned from your drawings to design new visualization algorithms. The results of this study will eventually help brain scientists examine pathological conditions related to disease treatment and diagnosis. Before we begin, please test your computer to ensure it supports the rendering. You can proceed if the left image and the right image match.
